Our Trips

Choose from a variety of rafting adventures, each designed to provide a unique and thrilling experience.

Trip Overview

We offer a range of whitewater rafting trips, from half-day excursions to multi-day river expeditions. All trips are led by experienced guides.

  • Half Day Adventure: Perfect for beginners and those short on time.
  • Full Day Excursion: Experience a full day of rafting with lunch included.
  • River Expedition: Multi-day trips for the ultimate rafting experience.

Sample Itinerary: River Expedition (5 Days)

Day 1: Arrive at the river and begin our journey.

Day 2: Navigate challenging rapids and enjoy scenic views.

Day 3: Explore hidden canyons and enjoy riverside camping.

Day 4: Experience the thrill of the river's biggest rapids.

Day 5: Conclude our trip with a final rafting stretch and farewell.

What's Included
  • Professional guide service
  • All necessary rafting equipment
  • Meals (as specified in itinerary)
  • Transportation to/from the river
What to Bring
  • Waterproof clothing
  • Sturdy footwear
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Camera (waterproof case recommended)
